# Mission

SKSecurity exists to provide enterprise-grade threat detection, behavioral analysis, and auto-quarantine for AI agent ecosystems.

It enables agents and operators to defend sovereign infrastructure with multi-source threat intelligence, real-time monitoring, and automated response — without depending on third-party security vendors.

Within the SKCapstone ecosystem, SKSecurity is the protection layer — it watches the perimeter so that agents running on CapAuth identity and SKComm transport operate in a hardened environment.
